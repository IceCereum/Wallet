- To see a list of your wallets, type "wallet list"
- To load your wallet in, type "wallet load <wallet_name>
- To see a list of your nicks, type "nick list"
- To transfer some funds, type "transfer" (for interactive) or
"transfer -to <to_addr> -a <amount> -msg 'message about transaction'
- To see your balance, type "balance"
- To sync your transactions, type "sync"; If you have new transactions, you
will have to enter a message for each new one.
- To see your transactions, type "transactions"

- To get all commands, type "help"
- To get help about a particular command, type "help <command>"

- To quit, type "quit"
